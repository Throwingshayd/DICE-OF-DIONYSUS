# 🎲 New Boons Implementation Summary

**Total Boons: 31** (10 original + 21 new)

## 🌟 **EPIC TIER BOONS** (8g) - Game Changers

### **Sisyphus' Boulder** 
- **God:** Sisyphus
- **Effect:** +5 Pips for every time you've rerolled this turn. Resets each turn.
- **Theme:** Endless effort and punishment - the more you roll, the stronger you become
- **Dynamic Display:** Shows current pips bonus based on rerolls

### **Hephaestus' Forge** ✨ *NEW NAME*
- **God:** Hephaestus (Master Craftsman)
- **Effect:** Pairs (2 of same number) count as Three of a Kind
- **Theme:** The forge transforms duplicates into triplicates
- **Former Name:** Echo of Narcissus → Now properly aligned with crafting/Three of a Kind theme
- **Mechanic:** Massive scoring flexibility

### **Kronos' Hourglass**
- **God:** Kronos (Titan of Time)
- **Effect:** Gain +2 Rolls permanently, but score threshold increases by 20% each Ante
- **Theme:** Time manipulation with consequences
- **Risk/Reward:** Perfect Balatro-style tradeoff

### **The Fates' Loom**
- **God:** The Fates (Clotho, Lachesis, Atropos)
- **Effect:** All dice that show consecutive numbers give ×3 Favour instead of ×2
- **Theme:** The three sisters weave destiny in patterns
- **Synergies:** Hermes (Small Straight), Apollo (Large Straight)

### **Pandora's Jar**
- **God:** Pandora
- **Effect:** Every 3rd turn, randomly destroy a Boon and gain ×4 Favour for that turn
- **Theme:** High risk/high reward chaos
- **Mechanic:** Forces adaptation and risk management

### **The Pantheon**
- **Effect:** For each unique god represented in your Boons, gain +0.5 Favour
- **Theme:** Collective power of all the gods
- **Dynamic Display:** Shows favour based on unique gods
- **Synergy:** Rewards diversity in boon collection

## 💙 **VIBRANT TIER BOONS** (5g) - Interesting Mechanics

### **Demeter's Harvest**
- **God:** Demeter (Goddess of Agriculture)
- **Effect:** Each turn, one random die permanently gains +1 to its value (max 9)
- **Theme:** Gradual growth and cultivation
- **Unique:** Permanent upgrades without libations!

### **Medusa's Gaze**
- **God:** Medusa (Gorgon)
- **Effect:** Any die showing 6 cannot be rerolled (acts as automatic hold)
- **Theme:** Turning things to stone (holding them in place)
- **Double-Edged:** Can be blessing or curse

### **Dionysus' Revelry**
- **God:** Dionysus (God of Wine and Chaos)
- **Effect:** After scoring, randomly set one die to a random value 1-6 for next turn
- **Theme:** Chaos and unpredictability
- **Fun Factor:** High excitement!

### **Apollo's Oracle**
- **God:** Apollo (God of Prophecy)
- **Effect:** Before rolling, see what the next roll will be. Can choose to skip it
- **Theme:** Perfect information and foresight
- **Strategic:** Enables masterful planning

### **Hydra's Heads**
- **God:** Hydra (Many-headed serpent)
- **Effect:** Whenever you score with exactly 2 dice, gain +30 Pips
- **Theme:** Two heads working together
- **Strategy:** Conflicts with "use all dice" strategies

### **Tantalus' Curse**
- **God:** Tantalus (Eternally Punished)
- **Effect:** +1 Favour for each gold you have, but cannot spend gold while active
- **Theme:** Wealth you cannot touch - eternal punishment
- **Dynamic Display:** Shows favour based on current gold
- **Risk:** Can't buy anything!

### **Pegasus' Flight**
- **God:** Pegasus (Winged Horse)
- **Effect:** Dice with values 5+ give ×0.5 extra Favour when scored
- **Theme:** Soaring high values
- **Synergy:** Pairs with high-value strategies

### **Cerberus' Watch**
- **God:** Cerberus (Three-headed Guardian)
- **Effect:** The first 3 dice you hold each turn gain +5 Pips each when scored
- **Theme:** The three-headed guardian protects what you hold
- **Strategy:** Makes hold order matter

### **Orpheus' Lyre**
- **God:** Orpheus (Musician)
- **Effect:** Scoring the same category twice in a row gives ×2 Favour the second time
- **Theme:** Repeating melodies
- **Risk:** Uses up scorecard faster

### **The Trojan Horse**
- **Effect:** After Turn 10, all your Boons give ×2 their normal effect
- **Theme:** Hidden power revealed when time is right
- **Strategy:** Survive to turn 10, then dominate

### **Golden Touch** *Enhanced Midas*
- **God:** Midas
- **Effect:** Interest is calculated at 1 gold per 3 saved (instead of 5)
- **Theme:** Everything turns to gold
- **Economy:** Makes saving more viable

## 🟤 **RUSTIC TIER BOONS** (3g) - Simple but Effective

### **Lucky Dice Bag**
- **Effect:** Whenever you roll a 1, reroll that die automatically (once per die per turn)
- **Theme:** Bad luck protection
- **Simple:** Just works!

### **Weighted Dice**
- **Effect:** +1 to all dice values when scoring (after rolling complete)
- **Theme:** Loaded dice
- **Reliable:** Always good, no conditions

### **Philosopher's Stone**
- **Effect:** Convert +3 Favour into +1 Gold when scoring (automatic)
- **Theme:** Alchemical transformation
- **Flexible:** Favour → Gold conversion

### **Gambler's Charm**
- **Effect:** 50% chance to gain +2 Gold when scoring, 50% chance to gain nothing
- **Theme:** Pure randomness
- **Fun:** Slot machine feel!

### **Marathon Runner**
- **Effect:** Gain +2 Pips per turn completed this Ante (stacks)
- **Theme:** Pheidippides' legendary run from Marathon to Athens
- **Dynamic Display:** Shows current pips bonus
- **Scaling:** Turn 1: +2, Turn 5: +10, Turn 13: +26
