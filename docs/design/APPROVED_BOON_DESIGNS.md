## 💎 EPIC TIER BOONS (8g)

### **1. Pandora's Jar** ❌ REJECTED
- **Cost:** 8g
- **Effect:** Every 3rd turn, randomly destroy a Boon and gain ×4 Favour for that turn
- **Mechanic:** High risk/high reward, forces adaptation

### **2. Echo of Narcissus** (became Hephaestus' Forge)
- **Cost:** 8g
- **Effect:** Pairs (2 of same number) count as Three of a Kind
- **Mechanic:** Makes lower hands easier to hit

### **3. Kronos' Hourglass**
- **Cost:** 8g (changed to 9g in implementation)
- **Effect:** Gain +2 Rolls permanently, but score reduced by 20%
- **Mechanic:** More chances but harder game
- **Status:** ✅ IMPLEMENTED (with price change)

### **4. Parmenides Die**
- **Cost:** 8g
- **Effect:** Scores swap between upper and lower pantheon (Ones↔Three of a Kind, Twos↔Small Straight, etc.)
- **Mechanic:** Pantheon swap by position; scoring in a category places the result in the corresponding slot
- **Status:** ✅ IMPLEMENTED

### **5. Ascetic's Vow**
- **Cost:** 8g
- **Effect:** If you have empty other Boon slots, gain +×1 Favour for each
- **Mechanic:** Solo boon challenge
- **Status:** ✅ IMPLEMENTED

### **6. Bellows of War**
- **Cost:** 8g
- **Effect:** Three/Four of Kind categories score as if you had one more matching die
- **Mechanic:** Virtual die (Three of Kind counts as Four!)
- **Status:** ✅ IMPLEMENTED

### **7. Carillon of the Muses**
- **Cost:** 5g (listed as Epic but priced as Vibrant)
- **Effect:** If all 5 dice have enhancements, gain ×3 Favour (secret: if all same enhancement, ×5 Favour)
- **Mechanic:** Enhancement collection game
- **Status:** ✅ IMPLEMENTED

### **8. Echo Chamber** (same as Reflection of Narcissus)
- **Cost:** 8g
- **Effect:** Boons trigger twice, but you have -2 rolls per turn
- **Mechanic:** Double effects, less rolls
- **Status:** ✅ IMPLEMENTED (as Reflection of Narcissus)

## 🔵 VIBRANT TIER BOONS (5g)

### **9. Sisyphus' Boulder**
- **Cost:** 8g (Vibrant but Epic price)
- **Effect:** +5 Pips for every time you've rerolled this turn. Resets each turn.
- **Mechanic:** Rewards using all your rolls
- **Status:** ✅ IMPLEMENTED

### **10. Demeter's Harvest**
- **Cost:** 5g
- **Effect:** Each turn, one random die permanently gains +1 to its value (max 9)
- **Mechanic:** Gradual power creep, dice evolve over game
- **Status:** ✅ IMPLEMENTED

### **11. Medusa's Gaze**
- **Cost:** 5g
- **Effect:** Any die showing 6 cannot be rerolled (acts as automatic hold) + lower sanctum scores give ×0.5 favour bonus
- **Mechanic:** Forces you to work with high rolls
- **Status:** ✅ IMPLEMENTED

### **12. Dionysus' Revelry**
- **Cost:** 5g
- **Effect:** After scoring, randomly set all die faces on one die to random values 1-6 for next turn
- **Mechanic:** Chaos element, ruins planning but exciting
- **Status:** ✅ IMPLEMENTED

### **13. Hydra's Heads**
- **Cost:** 5g
- **Effect:** Whenever you score with exactly 2 dice, gain +3 Favour
- **Mechanic:** Rewards minimalist scoring
- **Status:** ✅ IMPLEMENTED

### **14. Tantalus' Curse**
- **Cost:** 5g
- **Effect:** +0.5 Favour for each gold you have, but cannot spend gold while active
- **Mechanic:** Lock gold for power
- **Status:** ✅ IMPLEMENTED

### **15. Pegasus' Flight**
- **Cost:** 5g
- **Effect:** Dice with values 6+ give ×0.5 extra Favour when scored
- **Mechanic:** Rewards high dice specifically
- **Status:** ✅ IMPLEMENTED

### **16. Cerberus' Watch**
- **Cost:** 5g
- **Effect:** The first 3 dice you hold each turn gain +3 Pips each when scored
- **Mechanic:** Rewards selective holding
- **Status:** ✅ IMPLEMENTED

### **17. Olympian Council**
- **Cost:** 5g
- **Effect:** If you have 3+ Boons with the same god, they all give ×2 effect
- **Mechanic:** Rewards focus (opposite of Pantheon)
- **Status:** ❌ NOT IMPLEMENTED

### **18. The Symposium**
- **Cost:** 5g
- **Effect:** Each 4 of a kind or greater of matching dice gives +×1 Favour
- **Mechanic:** Passive favour generation
- **Status:** ✅ IMPLEMENTED

### **19. Divine Synergy**
- **Cost:** 8g (Vibrant but Epic price)
- **Effect:** Boons of the same rarity amplify each other (+5 Pips per matching rarity)
- **Mechanic:** Build mono-rarity decks
- **Status:** ✅ IMPLEMENTED

### **20. Reverse Polarity**
- **Cost:** 5g
- **Effect:** Odd numbers are worth double, even numbers worth half
- **Mechanic:** Completely changes strategy
- **Status:** ❌ NOT IMPLEMENTED

### **21. Symmetry**
- **Cost:** 5g
- **Effect:** If dice are palindromic (1-2-3-2-1), permanently gain +0.5 Favour each time
- **Mechanic:** Pattern recognition
- **Status:** ✅ IMPLEMENTED (with permanent favour gain)

### **22. Miser's Delight / Misery**
- **Cost:** 5g
- **Effect:** If you have 0 gold, gain ×2 Favour
- **Mechanic:** Poverty bonus
- **Status:** ✅ IMPLEMENTED (as Misery)

### **23. Alchemist's Delight / Mortal Vineyard**
- **Cost:** 5g
- **Effect:** Selling a Boon gives you a random Libation
- **Mechanic:** Convert resources
- **Status:** ✅ IMPLEMENTED (as Mortal Vineyard)

### **24. Proteus' Disguise**
- **Cost:** 5g
- **Effect:** Each turn, this Boon mimics the effect of a random other Boon you own
- **Mechanic:** Wildcard boon
- **Status:** ✅ IMPLEMENTED

### **25. Smog of Morpheus**
- **Cost:** 5g
- **Effect:** After final roll, all dice showing 2 or 4 become 3s
- **Mechanic:** Eliminates even numbers
- **Status:** ✅ IMPLEMENTED

### **26. The Zealot**
- **Cost:** 5g
- **Effect:** Gives +×1 favour of the most recently scored Worship card, to that score this Ante
- **Mechanic:** Worship synergy
- **Status:** ✅ IMPLEMENTED

### **27. The Investor / Cornucopia of Ploutos**
- **Cost:** 5g
- **Effect:** At end of Ante, gold ×1.5 (rounded down)
- **Mechanic:** Exponential gold growth
- **Status:** ✅ IMPLEMENTED (as Cornucopia of Ploutos)

### **28. The Completionist** ❌ NOT IN FILE
- **Cost:** 5g
- **Effect:** If you score in all 16 categories (including 7s/8s/9s), gain +500 score
- **Mechanic:** Long-term goal
- **Status:** ❌ NOT IMPLEMENTED

### **29. Message in a Bottle**
- **Cost:** 5g
- **Effect:** If you complete Ante with no other boons for entire ante, gain +50% of score threshold at end of ante
- **Mechanic:** Anti-collection challenge
- **Status:** ✅ IMPLEMENTED

### **30. Betrayal by Paris**
- **Cost:** 5g
- **Effect:** Destroy a random Boon at end of each Ante, gain +5 Gold
- **Mechanic:** Sacrificial economy
- **Status:** ✅ IMPLEMENTED

### **31. Eruption of Etna**
- **Cost:** 5g
- **Effect:** If 3+ Boons trigger on same turn, ×2 Favour
- **Mechanic:** Combo multiplier
- **Status:** ✅ IMPLEMENTED

### **32. The Cycle of Seasons**
- **Cost:** 5g
- **Effect:** When a Worship card triggers, also trigger a +1 favour to another god
- **Mechanic:** Cross-type synergy
- **Status:** ✅ IMPLEMENTED

### **33. Gold Standard**
- **Cost:** 8g (Vibrant but Epic price)
- **Effect:** All gold enhancements also give +3 Pips
- **Mechanic:** Supercharge gold faces
- **Status:** ✅ IMPLEMENTED

### **34. Doubling Season**

## 🟤 RUSTIC TIER BOONS (3g)

### **36. Lucky Dice Bag**
- **Cost:** 3g
- **Effect:** The first 1 rolled, reroll that die automatically (once per die per turn)
- **Mechanic:** Reduces bad luck
- **Status:** ✅ IMPLEMENTED

### **37. Gambler's Charm**
- **Cost:** 3g
- **Effect:** 50% chance to gain +2 Gold when scoring, 50% chance to lose 1 gold
- **Mechanic:** Pure randomness
- **Status:** ✅ IMPLEMENTED

### **38. Mathematician's Compass**
- **Cost:** 3g
- **Effect:** +10 Pips if your dice sum to an even number
- **Mechanic:** Simple condition
- **Status:** ✅ IMPLEMENTED

### **39. Assembly of Heroes**
- **Cost:** 3g
- **Effect:** If you have 5+ Boons, gain +15 Pips when scoring
- **Mechanic:** Rewards collecting
- **Status:** ✅ IMPLEMENTED

### **40. First Blood**
- **Cost:** 5g (Rustic but Vibrant price in file)
- **Effect:** Your first score each Ante gives +50 Pips
- **Mechanic:** Front-loaded power
- **Status:** ✅ IMPLEMENTED

### **41. Early Bird Gets the Worm**
- **Cost:** 3g
- **Effect:** Turns 1-3: +20 Pips. Turns 4-5: +2 gold. Turns 6-13: -5 Pips
- **Mechanic:** Front-loaded, then fades
- **Status:** ✅ IMPLEMENTED

### **42. Marathon Runner**
- **Cost:** 3g
- **Effect:** Gain +1 Pips per roll taken (stacks, destroyed when scratched or Pips reach 42+)
- **Mechanic:** Gets better over time
- **Status:** ✅ IMPLEMENTED

### **43. Midnight Oil**
- **Cost:** 5g (Rustic but Vibrant price)
- **Effect:** Turns 12-13 give +24 Pips but you lose 1 roll each turn during those turns
- **Mechanic:** Late-game boost with cost
- **Status:** ✅ IMPLEMENTED

### **44. Prime Time**
- **Cost:** 3g
- **Effect:** Prime number dice (2,3,5,7) give +5 Pips each
- **Mechanic:** Simple math condition
- **Status:** ✅ IMPLEMENTED (says +1 pip in implementation)

### **45. Reckless Abandon**
- **Cost:** 3g
- **Effect:** +50 Pips but you cannot hold dice
- **Mechanic:** Pure luck, no strategy
- **Status:** ✅ IMPLEMENTED

### **46. The Locksmith**
- **Cost:** 3g
- **Effect:** Held dice gain +1 pips for each roll they were held when scoring
- **Mechanic:** Holding becomes better
- **Status:** ✅ IMPLEMENTED

### **47. The Merchant**
- **Cost:** 3g
- **Effect:** Selling libation and worship cards gives +1 extra gold
- **Mechanic:** Better sell value
- **Status:** ✅ IMPLEMENTED

### **48. The Heretic**
- **Cost:** 3g
- **Effect:** Each turn gain +2 pips (stacks, resets at end of ante or when worship card is used)
- **Mechanic:** Boon-only challenge
- **Status:** ✅ IMPLEMENTED

### **49. Typhon**
- **Cost:** 3g
- **Effect:** Rolling all 1s on the first roll gives +90% of score threshold (incredibly rare)
- **Mechanic:** Jackpot condition
- **Status:** ✅ IMPLEMENTED

### **50. Nyxian Seduction**
